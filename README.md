I'm a recent graduate from QUT Gardens Point with a Bachelor of Information Technology in Computer Science. I also have minors in Intelligent Systems + Computational and Simulation Science.

I mostly work on Minecraft mods and datapacks in my free time. I'm in the process of updating all of them to 26.1.2 and publishing them on my [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/user/brainage04) profile - here's a list of mods I've made:
- [MagicCarpet](https://github.com/brainage04/MagicCarpet) - adds three tiers of Magic Carpets that you can fly around in - complete with balanced recipes, advancements, cool flying animations and more! Tip: 2 people can ride a Magic Carpet at once :)
- [VeinMiner](https://github.com/brainage04/VeinMiner) - a Vein Miner mod that works with veins of the same mineral but different stone types (e.g. a mix of Diamond Ore and Deepslate Diamond Ore)
- [MaxVillagerTrades](https://github.com/brainage04/MaxVillagerTrades) - modifies all enchantments on items and books offered by Villagers to always be max level. Makes getting expensive enchants like Sharpness V and Protection IV from Librarians much easier.
- [SimpleTPA](https://github.com/brainage04/SimpleTPA) - adds a "teleport ask" system to Minecraft, with a gamerule that allows auto-accepting of teleport requests for players on your auto-accept whitelist!
- [GetEnchantInfo](https://github.com/brainage04/GetEnchantInfo) - adds helpfun commands that tell you about enchantments (max level, what items they can be applied to, conflicting enchantments) and enchantable items (allowed enchantments including conflicting enchantments with max levels) to make maxing gear easier. Works with modded enchantments too!
- [SimpleTwitchChat](https://github.com/brainage04/SimpleTwitchChat) - integrates your Twitch chat with your Minecraft chat. Receive Twitch chat messages in your Minecraft chat, and send Twitch chat messages from your Minecraft chat using /tc!
- [MilkablePlayers](https://github.com/brainage04/MilkablePlayers) - allows you to milk players like cows. Very useful in multiplayer when raiding certain structures with a lot of mobs that give you negative effects on hit (e.g. Trial Chambers with Stray and Bogged spawners)
- [DevUtils](https://github.com/brainage04/DevUtils) - a texture atlas generator for Forge 1.8.9. Supports the vanilla texture atlas and the Hypixel SkyBlock texture atlas (5000+ custom items modelled as player heads)

Here's some mods I'm still working on:
- [ProceduralDungeon](https://github.com/brainage04/ProceduralDungeon) - adds a procedurally generated dungeon that has multiple different difficulty/loot tiers and themes across all three vanilla dimensions. Major work in progress.
- [FortniteInMinecraft](https://github.com/brainage04/FortniteInMinecraft) - adds Fortnite mechanics to Minecraft. This includes building, editing, guns, consumables and more! Major work in progress.
- [BrainageHUD](https://github.com/brainage04/BrainageHUD) - a highly customisable heads up display mod that adds many different widgets - position, armour, keystrokes, reach display, network stats, and much more! Also comes with an easy to use drag and drop editor :) (if you're interested in a library that makes developing HUD mods much easier, that also has a build-in drag and drop editor, check out [HudRendererLib](https://github.com/brainage04/HudRendererLib))
- [ToggleSprint](https://github.com/brainage04/ToggleSprint) - a Forge 1.8.9 version of BrainageHUD, with a toggle sprint feature that is safe to use on Hypixel. I play on the latest version with Fabric way more than I play on Forge 1.8.9, so there's a bunch of features from BrainageHUD that I need to add to this mod.
- [IceSkates](https://github.com/brainage04/IceSkates) - adds Ice Skates and Roller Skates, which basically let you glide on Ice and regular terrain respectively as if you were surfing on a boat. Ice Skates are super fast on ice and super slow otherwise, Roller Skates are equally fast on ice and otherwise.
- [Telekinesis](https://github.com/brainage04/Telekinesis) - automatically teleports mob/block drops into your inventory, like the [Telekinesis](https://wiki.hypixel.net/Telekinesis_Enchantment) enchant from Hypixel SkyBlock
- [BrainageMinigames](https://github.com/brainage04/BrainageMinigames) / [BrainageUHC](https://github.com/brainage04/BrainageUHC) (i need to combine these two into one) - adds a bunch of minigames hosted in completely separate dimensions that I made so that I could play minigames with my friends on my servers without having to switch worlds or make a mess of an existing world
- [TextureAtlasGenerator](https://github.com/brainage04/TextureAtlasGenerator) - a Fabric 1.21 port of the texture atlas generator from DevUtils. Been smashing my head against a wall with this one for dozens of hours - it may take a while.
